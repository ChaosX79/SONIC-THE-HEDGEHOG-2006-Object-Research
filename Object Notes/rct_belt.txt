The Radical Train Conveyer Belts that change direction just to be annoying.

First parameter (a single) controls how fast the belt moves when moving in its forward direction.
Second parameter (a single) controls how fast the belt moves when moving in its backwards direction.
Third parameter (a single) controls how long it takes before the Conveyer Belt changes which direction its moving in.
Fourth parameter (a single) is unknown, possibly some form of identifier?
