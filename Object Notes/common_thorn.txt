Panels of Spikes.

First parameter (a signed 32 bit integer) controls how the spikes move.
1 = Spikes will permantly stay up.
2 = Spikes will alternate between going up and down.
3 = Spikes will permantly stay down.
Second parameter (a single) controls how long the spikes stay up for.
Third parameter (a single) controls how long the spikes stay down for.
